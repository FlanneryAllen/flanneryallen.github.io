# Webex Orbit

Also known as: "On Demand Creation of Contextually Relevant and Location-Aware Ephemeral Services"

## Problem Statement

The internet makes the whole world accessible, but sometimes interaction needs to focus on the here and now - the people and location services available in this moment. Existing solutions require downloading multiple venue-specific apps or manually connecting through social platforms like Facebook or Twitter.

## Innovation

Automatic creation of location-based feed containing contextual chats and micro-local services that are:
- **Ephemeral** - appear when you enter, disappear when you leave
- **Curated** - location owner controls available services
- **Personalized** - filtered by user preferences and permissions

## Technical Approach

A location-based, pop-up social platform that is both public and private. The system:

1. Detects user entering a location (physically or virtually)
2. Automatically adds user to geo-based communication spaces
3. Surfaces location-relevant app functionality without requiring full app download
4. Creates dynamic policy-based framework at intersection of user preferences and location owner curation
5. Removes spaces and services when user exits or time expires

**Key Technical Components:**
- Quality of Position (QoP) weighting system
- Adaptive ID for privacy control
- Location-first browser
- Recommendation engine
- OpenRoaming integration for automatic authentication
- DNA Spaces for backend administration

## Use Cases

- Retail: Access store services without downloading 182MB Walmart app
- Stadiums: Vote on encore songs, polls, interact with performers
- Airports: Interactive maps, real-time updates, gate information
- Restaurants: Menu access, loyalty rewards without dedicated app
- Workplace: Book conference rooms, create community

## Novel Features

- Geo-based ephemeral communication spaces that auto-create and auto-delete
- Divorcing location-aware functionality from venue apps
- Policy-based framework balancing user privacy with venue curation
- Universal interface (&amp; symbol) for location-based experiences
- "Discovery as a Service" - exposing relevant app slices without download friction

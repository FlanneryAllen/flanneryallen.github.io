# You May Be on Mute, But Your Body Language Isn't

Also titled "On Mute. Not on Pause."

Internal communications article for Cisco Employee Connection addressing remote work dynamics and digital communication skills.

## Publication

Cisco Employee Connection (internal communications platform)

## Approach

Turning remote work guidance into engaging narrative that respects employees as thinking adults rather than delivering corporate policy.

## Theme

Exploring how communication continues even when audio is muted - the importance of presence, engagement, and non-verbal signals in digital workspaces.

## Context

Part of communications strategy supporting 75,000 employees through digital transformation and remote work transitions.

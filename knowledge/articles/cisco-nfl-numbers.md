# The Numbers Behind the Game: Cisco and the NFL

Published article on Cisco Newsroom highlighting the company's partnership with the NFL through compelling statistics and storytelling.

## Publication

Cisco Newsroom, February 5, 2025

## Key Statistics Featured

- NFL generates over $18 billion in revenue annually
- 200 million people globally watch the Super Bowl each year
- During Super Bowl LVIII, Cisco blocked connection attempts from blacklisted regions
- Stopped 39,000 security intelligence events during single game
- Supports all 32 NFL teams year-round
- 24/7 operations maintaining connectivity and security

## Approach

Using data-driven storytelling to make enterprise technology tangible:
- Leading with compelling numbers
- Translating invisible infrastructure work into understandable impact
- Showing how cybersecurity success means fans see nothing go wrong

## Theme

"Cisco's most important victories remain invisible to fans—blocking thousands of threats while maintaining network reliability so viewers experience uninterrupted gameplay."

## Partnership Context

Cisco became Official Enterprise Networking Partner and Cybersecurity Partner of the NFL in 2019, supporting league-wide infrastructure.

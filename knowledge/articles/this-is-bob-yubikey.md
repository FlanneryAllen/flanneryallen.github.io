# This is Bob. He just got a YubiKey.

Internal communications article for Cisco Employee Connection demonstrating approach to security awareness through narrative and human-centered storytelling.

## Publication

Cisco Employee Connection (internal communications platform)

## Approach

Making security technology relatable and approachable for 75,000 employees by:
- Using narrative instead of corporate speak
- Creating memorable character-driven messaging
- Translating technical security concepts into human stories

## Context

Part of broader global communications strategy bringing agency thinking to internal employee communications at Cisco.

# Global Communications Strategy

Internal communications strategy bringing agency thinking to Cisco's global employee communications for 75,000 employees.

## Approach

Brought agency thinking to Cisco's internal communications:
- Turned announcements into campaigns
- Policy rollouts into stories
- Corporate speak into human voice

## Scope

75,000 employees globally through:
- Digital transformation initiatives
- Return-to-office transitions
- Constant organizational change

## Philosophy

Treated employees like customers: with respect, clarity, and human voice. Applied external marketing and campaign thinking to internal communications.

## Impact

Employees responded to internal communications the way customers respond when organizations respect them - with engagement, understanding, and adoption.

## Role

Global communications strategist at Cisco, applying agency methodology to internal employee experience.

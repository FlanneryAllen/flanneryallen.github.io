# Cisco AI Messaging Framework

Strategic messaging framework built in 2023 to align Cisco's response to the ChatGPT moment and position the company coherently across all business units.

## Challenge

How do you align 20+ business units during the ChatGPT moment? Twenty business units, dozens of executives, hundreds of content touchpoints, all needing coherent messaging about AI.

## Solution

Built comprehensive messaging framework that kept Cisco coherent by:
- Shifting conversation from "what AI is" to "what AI does for humans"
- Creating alignment structure across business units
- Providing foundation for hundreds of content touchpoints

## Scope

- 20+ business units
- Dozens of executive stakeholders
- Hundreds of content touchpoints across cisco.com, marketing, sales enablement

## Impact

Framework became foundation for:
- Cisco's AI Readiness Index
- cisco.com/ai positioning
- Cross-portfolio AI messaging consistency

## Approach

Strategic messaging that translates complex technology into human-centered narrative, ensuring consistency while allowing business units to maintain their specific value propositions.

## Role

Messaging strategist and framework architect at Cisco

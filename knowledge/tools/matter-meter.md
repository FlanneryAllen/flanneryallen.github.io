# Matter Meter

A standalone tool developed in 2025 for identifying what matters in organizational narratives and story systems.

## Purpose

Every storyteller needs to know what matters. The Matter Meter analyzes narrative content to surface the elements that carry weight, drive meaning, and shape audience understanding.

## Development

Built as one of three storytelling prototypes in 2025. The three tools (Matter Meter, Story Gap Mapper, Story Signal) represent distinct storytelling moves that were later integrated into the Narrative Agent.

## Use Cases

- Identifying high-impact narrative elements in content
- Prioritizing story components for strategic messaging
- Understanding what audiences will remember and respond to
- Focusing communication on elements that matter most

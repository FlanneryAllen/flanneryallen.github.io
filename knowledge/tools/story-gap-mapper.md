# Story Gap Mapper

A standalone tool developed in 2025 for identifying gaps, omissions, and missing elements in organizational story systems.

## Purpose

Every storyteller needs to find what's missing. The Story Gap Mapper analyzes narrative structures to reveal where stories are incomplete, where connections are missing, and what's not being said.

## Development

Built as one of three storytelling prototypes in 2025. The three tools (Matter Meter, Story Gap Mapper, Story Signal) represent distinct storytelling moves that were later integrated into the Narrative Agent.

## Use Cases

- Identifying missing narrative connections between products and positioning
- Surfacing gaps in organizational messaging across business units
- Finding unstated assumptions that create confusion
- Mapping what audiences need to hear but aren't hearing

# Story Signal

A standalone tool developed in 2025 for detecting emerging patterns and anticipating where narratives are heading.

## Purpose

Every storyteller needs to spot what's coming. Story Signal analyzes narrative momentum and structure to surface signals about where stories are going and what's emerging next.

## Development

Built as one of three storytelling prototypes in 2025. The three tools (Matter Meter, Story Gap Mapper, Story Signal) represent distinct storytelling moves that were later integrated into the Narrative Agent.

## Use Cases

- Detecting emerging narrative patterns in organizational communications
- Anticipating where customer conversations are heading
- Identifying early signals in market messaging
- Understanding narrative momentum before it becomes obvious

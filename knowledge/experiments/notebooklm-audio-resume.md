# NotebookLM Audio Résumé

AI-generated podcast experiment created for Netflix application using Google's NotebookLM to synthesize portfolio content and job requirements.

## Platform

Google NotebookLM

## Approach

Fed portfolio content and Netflix job description to NotebookLM, which generated conversational podcast analyzing alignment between experience and role requirements.

## Purpose

Experimental application format demonstrating:
- AI tool implementation skills
- Ability to synthesize complex information
- Creative approach to showcasing fit
- Understanding of emerging AI capabilities

## Format

Audio podcast discussing why portfolio experience maps to Netflix role requirements, generated entirely by AI based on source materials.

## Outcome

October 2024 experiment showing the best time to test new approaches is before you need them. Demonstrated both technical capability and willingness to experiment with emerging tools.

# Narrative Agent

A patent-pending system that reads an organization's full story and finds gaps, spots misalignment, and shows what's actually missing using narrative algebra.

## Overview

The Narrative Agent applies narrative algebra—a mathematical approach to storytelling—to analyze organizational narratives at scale. It identifies structural gaps, misalignments between stated intent and actual messaging, and missing narrative elements that prevent coherent communication.

## Status

Provisional patent filed February 2026. Infrastructure powers the three storytelling tools (Matter Meter, Story Gap Mapper, Story Signal) also developed in 2025.

## Technical Approach

Uses narrative algebra as the underlying framework for:
- Reading complete organizational story corpus
- Identifying narrative gaps and inconsistencies
- Mapping alignment between different story layers
- Surfacing what's missing from the narrative architecture

## Use Cases

- Finding messaging inconsistencies across multiple business units
- Mapping narrative integrity in organizational communications
- Identifying structural gaps in product positioning
- Aligning technical capabilities with customer-facing narratives

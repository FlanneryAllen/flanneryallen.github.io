# Julie Voice Skill - Test Example

## How to Use This Skill

In Claude, type: `/skill julie-voice`

Then provide your brief and Claude will write in your voice.

---

## Example Brief
"Write intro copy for my portfolio homepage"

## Example Output Using This Skill

**Before (Generic AI):**
"Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a creative professional with extensive experience in strategic communications and brand storytelling. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with Fortune 500 companies to craft compelling narratives that drive engagement and deliver measurable results."

**After (Julie Voice):**
"I write for humans stuck in corporations.

For eight years at Cisco, I turned IT policies into stories people actually read. Made employees care about security by creating a stick figure named Bob. Transformed a calendar mention into editorial that broke engagement records.

The work here started with a simple belief: employees deserve the same craft as customers do.

Sometimes that meant putting the intranet in the cafeteria. Sometimes it meant writing 500 words when they asked for 50. Always meant treating the audience like readers, not metrics.

Here's what that looked like."

## Another Example

**Brief:** "Write a bio for LinkedIn"

**Output:**
"I solve problems with words.

Currently helping companies tell better stories about their technology. Previously spent eight years at Cisco making internal communications feel less internal.

Created Bob (he's the stick figure who sold out YubiKeys globally). Wrote editorial instead of announcements. Put websites in cafeterias when emails stopped working.

The thread through all of it: finding the human story in corporate initiatives. Making complex simple without making it dumb. Proving that employees deserve the same creative standards as customers.

Patents: 2. Editorial pieces: 100+. Belief that good writing can change behavior: unwavering."

---

## Key Differences You'll Notice

1. **No em dashes** (replaced with periods and commas)
2. **Specific details** (stick figure named Bob, not "creative campaign")
3. **Real numbers** (100+, not "numerous")
4. **Active voice** throughout
5. **Short punchy sentences** mixed with longer explanatory ones
6. **Starts with action** not description
7. **Shows through examples** not adjectives

This skill captures your voice from the comms work, pricing documents, and website copy, blending warmth, clarity, and quiet confidence without AI tells.

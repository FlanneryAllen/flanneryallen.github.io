Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

TL;DR: This portfolio site doesn't collect, store, or track any personal information. No cookies, no analytics, no tracking. Third-party embeds (YouTube, Chatbase) may have their own privacy policies.

Overview

Your privacy is important. This portfolio website is designed to showcase my work without compromising your privacy. This policy explains what information (if any) is collected when you visit this site.

Information We Don't Collect

This website does not:

  • Use cookies or tracking cookies
  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics services (no Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Track your behavior across sites
  • Store any data about your visit
  • Use browser fingerprinting
  • Collect IP addresses

Third-Party Services

This site embeds content from third-party services that may collect their own data:

YouTube Videos

  • Embedded demo videos are hosted on YouTube
  • YouTube may collect data according to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Consider using YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode if concerned

Chatbase AI Chatbot

  • The AI Julie chatbot is powered by Chatbase
  • Conversations with the chatbot are processed by Chatbase
  • See Chatbase Privacy Policy

Google Fonts

  • Typography is loaded from Google Fonts CDN
  • Google may log font requests
  • No cookies are set by Google Fonts

GitHub Pages Hosting

  • This site is hosted on GitHub Pages
  • GitHub may collect basic server logs
  • See GitHub Privacy Statement

External Links

This site contains links to external websites. Once you leave this site, you're subject to the privacy policies of those external sites. All external links open in new tabs and include rel="noopener noreferrer" for security.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to secure. The site uses HTTPS encryption to ensure your connection is secure. Content Security Policy (CSP) headers prevent unauthorized scripts from running.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request, delete, or opt-out from. You can browse this site with confidence that your privacy is respected.

Browser Privacy Settings

For maximum privacy while browsing:

  • Use privacy-focused browsers (Firefox, Brave)
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser
  • Use ad blockers or privacy extensions
  • Browse in private/incognito mode
  • Disable third-party cookies in browser settings

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. The site will remain privacy-focused and will never add tracking without clear notice.

Open Source

This website's code is open source and available on GitHub. You can verify these privacy claims by reviewing the source code.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how this site handles privacy:

  • Review the source code on GitHub
  • Open an issue on the repository
  • Contact through professional channels listed on the portfolio
Privacy Promise: This portfolio will always prioritize your privacy. No tracking, no cookies, no compromises.

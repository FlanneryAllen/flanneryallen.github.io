## Core Principles (Hidden but guides all copy)

**No em dashes** — Using periods and commas instead
**Specific over general** — "75,000 people" not "tens of thousands"
**Show the thinking** — "The engineers had better code. I had a better question."
**Name the truth** — "Got tired of cover letters"
**Active voice** — "Built" not "was responsible for building"
**Contrast for clarity** — "From what AI is to what AI does for humans"
**Details that stick** — "Bob the stick figure"
**Credit without caveat** — "Co-founded with two engineers"

The Source Framework that became Cisco's AI narrative ▼
The Insight

ChatGPT made AI visible, tangible, accessible. Suddenly everyone understood. Especially boardrooms.

Cisco's sweet spot: AI for the Enterprise. We had the infrastructure. The security. The scale.

The Position

AI is amplification. Not replacement. Every capability magnifies what humans already do. Just bigger.

No single AI formula exists. It's about problems solved. Not methods used.

The Philosophy

Responsible AI is non-negotiable. Ethics, privacy, sustainability. Not features. Requirements.

Deep listening comes before deep learning. Understand the need. Then apply the tech.

The Line That Landed

The future will be one of compounding intelligence.

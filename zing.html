Entrepreneurship

Zing.

The feeling you get when you see something you love.

Zing began as a Branding and Design Studio that did the occasional Pop-Up Shop. Eventually, with some grit, a vision, and a strong business plan, Zing turned into a full-on lifestyle store with Interior Designers and inspiring workshops that usually included some wine.

The evolution of Zing into a lifestyle store was an accumulation and expression of our design careers thus far. It wasn't a replacement, just another extension. Design is a decision. Deciding the details that are going to come together to create a solution to a problem. It doesn't matter if it's on a piece of paper, on a website or in a living room.
